# High-Confidence Computing Submission Package Drafts

## 1. Cover Letter Draft

Dear Editor-in-Chief,

We are pleased to submit our manuscript entitled “TRUST-Swarm: A High-Confidence Graph-Temporal Intelligent Computing Framework for Secure Multi-UAV Mission Assurance Under Cyber-Physical Attacks” for consideration as a research article in High-Confidence Computing.

This manuscript presents TRUST-Swarm, a high-confidence intelligent computing framework for secure multi-UAV mission assurance under cyber-physical attacks. The framework integrates graph-temporal mission-state recognition, calibrated confidence evaluation, out-of-distribution cyber-physical stress testing, perturbation-based traceability, and recovery-oriented mission reasoning.

The work is aligned with the scope of High-Confidence Computing because it addresses secure intelligent computing, trusted cyber-physical mission assurance, confidence-aware decision support, traceable prediction evidence, and active defense-oriented reasoning. Rather than positioning the proposed model as only a classifier, the manuscript emphasizes high-confidence computing mechanisms required for secure autonomous UAV swarm operations.

The manuscript reports a three-seed simulation study covering normal operation, jamming, spoofing, telemetry tampering, and combined cyber-physical attack states. It further evaluates calibration, OOD vulnerability, explainability, and recovery reasoning. The results show that secure UAV swarm mission assurance requires more than high classification accuracy; it requires reliable confidence estimation, distribution-shift awareness, traceable decision evidence, and recovery-oriented response support.

This manuscript has not been published elsewhere and is not under consideration by another journal. All authors have approved the submission. We believe the manuscript will be of interest to readers of High-Confidence Computing working on secure intelligent systems, cyber-physical security, trustworthy AI, autonomous systems, and high-confidence mission assurance.

Thank you for considering our submission.

## 2. Highlights Draft

* A high-confidence graph-temporal intelligent computing framework is proposed for secure multi-UAV mission assurance.
* TRUST-Swarm integrates mission-state recognition, calibration, OOD stress testing, traceable explanation, and recovery reasoning.
* Cyber-physical attacks including jamming, spoofing, tampering, and combined attacks are evaluated.
* OOD stress tests expose mission-risk degradation under unseen stealth jamming, GPS drift, tampering, and delayed combined attacks.
